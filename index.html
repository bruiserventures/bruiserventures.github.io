Converting ideas into businesses

Bruiser Ventures is a venture capital fund that invests with entrepreneurs working in data science, analytics, machine learning, and data architecture/infrastructure. We excel at serving not as just a source of funding, but also as a startup incubator and advisement partner.

We've watched as the trend in the startup environment of well-seasoned VC partners taking advantage of less politically minded entrepreneurs has gotten worse. That's not us: we're ex-academics with strong technical expertise and backgrounds that believe in the best idea winning, being fair, and caring solely about seeing new, awesome tech making it to market. So, we started Bruiser Ventures to do that. We're a tough guy, and we fight battles for our entrepreneur partners, not for ourselves or the fund's equity partners.
