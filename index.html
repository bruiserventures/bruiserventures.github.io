Converting ideas into businesses

Bruiser Ventures is a venture capital fund that invests with entrepreneurs working in data science, analytics, machine learning, and data architecture/infrastructure. We believe a VC should empower entrepreneurs and obsess over product instead of RoI. That's us: we're ex-academics with technical and business expertise that believe in the best idea winning, being fair, and caring solely about seeing new, awesome product making it to market. We started Bruiser Ventures to do that.
